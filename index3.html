About Geeta University

Geeta University was established in 1995 and is recognized by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the Government of Haryana. It is one of the leading universities in Northern India.

The university spans over 50 acres of green campus with modern classrooms, smart labs, sports facilities, hostels, and a central library with over 1,00,000 books and digital resources.

Our mission is to develop competent, ethical, and innovative professionals who contribute meaningfully to society and the global economy.
